Why Mystic Warrior?

Fast

With over 190 commands and exceptional speed. In terms of speed, Mystic Warrior is one of the greatest bots. Powered by the Discord.JS module. The bot's speed is really fast when compared to other bots with this many commands.

Secure

The Mystic Warrior crew is committed towards data protection. Mystic Warrior does not store any of your personal information. We only gather information about your server and your IDs for command purposes. To learn more about our policy, please  run $privacy command.
